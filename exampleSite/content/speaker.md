# Speaker One-Pager

Gallo speaks from lived experience rather than polished distance: recovery, grief, masculinity, dating, kink, communication, and the messy work of becoming less self-destructive.

## Key Points

- Recovery and relapse-free life after loss
- Dating and relationships from a kink-aware perspective
- Communication that is direct, honest, and practical
- Public conversations shaped by lived experience rather than theory alone
- More than 25 guest podcast appearances

## Short Bio

Gallo is a writer, speaker, and former podcast host whose work explores relationships, recovery, desire, grief, and the stories people tell themselves to survive. His voice is candid, funny, and sometimes abrasive in exactly the way real life tends to be.
