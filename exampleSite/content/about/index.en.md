# Welcome

_Sucias are my Favorite_ started as a podcast about dating, relationships, life, and sex advice with a kink twist. The show is retired now, but the broader body of work remains: writing, guest appearances, and an archive of the original episodes.

This site is meant to hold that material in one place without platform weirdness getting in the way.

Gallo is a Texan of Mexican heritage whose life has moved through tragedy, recovery, service, homelessness, creative reinvention, and more than a few near catastrophes. He served in the U.S. Army, has worked across wildly different worlds, and eventually left corporate life to focus more directly on creative work.

His perspective comes through a mix of hard experience, kink community knowledge, recovery work, and a refusal to pretend people are simpler than they are.

## What Lives Here

- Archived episodes from the retired podcast
- Blog posts and personal writing
- Guest appearances on other shows
- A speaker page and contact information
